The Story of The Creator

Once upon a time, there was a young man trying to get his bachelors degree in software engineering. He went throughout college, taking many classes, but not many of them had to do with web application development. He was always interested in it, but didn't really have time to spend on trying it out. UNTIL, his senior project came. He was told to make something that he hasn't made before, use technology that he doesn't know about. Thus the meal plan generator was born. From the idea of his brother, he wanted to make something that solved the problem that plagued his family. Coming up with what to have for dinner. Now, his family can spend less time debating and more time enjoying delicous food.
